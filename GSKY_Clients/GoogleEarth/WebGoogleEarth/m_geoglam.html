  • Choose the GSKY layer.
  • Choose the region.
  • Create the KML.
  • Click the link for KML.
  • Open in "Google Earth".
  • For unlisted regions:
    • Click the map icon.
    • Move the crosshair to position.
    • Click 'GDAL' at the bottom of map.
    • Choose the size of area (1 to 25).
    • Click the map icon again.
  • Notes:
    • Too many layers will crash the app.
    • Multiple dates do not work.
    • Must have Google Earth App.
    • Must be VPN connected to NCI.
